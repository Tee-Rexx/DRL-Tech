Portfolio

Asset Dash

AssetDash primarily targets investors with holdings in multiple assets — NFTs and crypto traders who may require several tracking apps to keep tabs on their positions. You can also track your stocks, mutual funds and bank balances on the platform. Suppose you have crypto assets and NFTs stored on diverse networks and blockchains on your virtual portfolio. AssetDash can help you keep track of them. .

Kubera

Kubera is a modern one-stop-shop financial tracker, helping its users track net worth and investment returns for all key asset classes. With Kubera, you can potentially track cryptocurrencies, stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, global currencies, precious metals, private equity, REITs and assets like URL value and vehicles. It helps you understand your investment by showing a comprehensive picture of your total finance in a single point.

Ziggma

Ziggma is a comprehensive investment management, stock analysis and portfolio tracking platform that helps self-directed investors improve performance through algorithm-powered big data stock research and innovative portfolio management tools. Ziggma serves two core objectives — it helps investors understand their portfolio through its robust management tools and provides them with high-quality quantitative stock research at low cost.
